You can click on the thumbnail pictures to view and manipulate the graphs after you have downloaded and installed either the full version of DPGraph or the free DPGraph Viewer. If your version of DPGraph or the DPGraph Viewer can't display a graph then you may need to update to the most recent version. To view the commands that created the graphs, click on Edit inside DPGraph or the Viewer. Some versions of Netscape require you to stop and restart your browser after installing. Feel free to use these graphs in any way you'd like, as long as you give credit to the contributor. Due to time constraints, we are currently not accepting new contributions to the Math Art Gallery.
